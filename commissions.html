Commissions

Apart from my own projects, I am also open for commissions, be that for other mod projects, private packs or anything inbetween.
To get a feel for what I do and have been doing in the past, feel free to look around this website or the Community Discord Server.

Pricing:

Textures:

Between €1 and €10, per item (meaning per texture), depending on complexity. Simple textures for single blocks or items cost less than GUI textures or entity atlases.

Models:

Starting at €3 for simple models, the more complex a model gets, the more expensive it will be.

Larger projects:

Priced individually, based on scope and complexity, calculated on estimated work time. I'll give you a quote before starting.
Revisions and turnaround time depend on project size and will be discussed upfront.

Currency & Payment:

All prices are listed in EUR (€). Payments made in other currencies must matc hthe EUR amount after conversion. Any resulting differences or fees are to be covered by the client.
Payment details will be discussed upon reaching an agreement on the commission.
