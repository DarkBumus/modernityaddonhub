About Modernity Addon Hub

Welcome to the Modernity Addon Hub! This project originally began with the idea of allowing functionality similar to Vanilla Tweaks for older (more specifically 1.7.10) versions of Minecraft. Instead of hosting the files on the community Discord server, as before, they will now be made available here. Most tweaks are realized using the MCPatcher mod, which is commonly found in OptiFine or similar mods and has recently been completely remade for 1.7.10. Should you be unsure as to how to create your very own, usable Texture Pack with the Addons you'd like to use, feel free to consult the 'Help' button in the top right, or come visit our Discord server!

This project's contents will always be free for everyone, however if you wish to support the project financially, you can do so on Ko-fi. I would be beyond delighted!
